About

Reflective Summary

Gibbs' Reflective Cycle

Using Gibbs' reflective cycle, I review the employability skills I built during the Critical and Creative Thinking module. I joined workshops, idea generation tasks, and group discussions. At first I felt unsure about sharing ideas and challenging set views. The sessions went well once I prepared and listened to my peers. My critical thinking and creativity grew through practice and feedback. Analysing my progress, I see real gains in problem solving, communication, time management, and digital research. I learned to question assumptions before reaching conclusions. Setbacks taught me to test more options rather than rush a single answer. I also became more confident presenting ideas to a group. Looking back, the module sharpened skills employers value and showed gaps I still need to close. My action plan is to practise structured problem solving, build a stronger online portfolio, and seek work experience in human resources to keep developing further overall.
